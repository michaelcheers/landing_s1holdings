Our Strategy

Focused capital, lasting impact

S1 Holdings was founded with a clear mission: to build a leading portfolio of B2B services businesses through thoughtful acquisitions and hands-on operational stewardship.

Our approach is focused and deliberate. We target 1 to 2 acquisitions per year, ensuring cultural alignment, operational stability, and long-term growth potential in every deal.

We identify and acquire established B2B services companies with strong fundamentals, loyal client bases, and proven management teams.

02

Operate

A dedicated Managing Director leads each business unit, preserving company culture while driving performance and operational improvement.

03

Compound

We reinvest in our portfolio and pursue strategic add-ons, compounding trust, talent, and returns over the long term.
